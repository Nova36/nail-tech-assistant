04 — Logo Concepts

Direction G — Bottle Silhouette + Wordmark

G1 — Classic Bottle + Wordmark Tall straight-sided bottle in Mulberry Dusk; Fraunces Light wordmark beside.
G2 — Faceted Bottle + Stacked Wordmark Recommended Primary Angular bottle in Mulberry Dusk with Dusty Rose cap; wordmark stacked in two lines.

Direction H — Bottle Letterform Substitution

H1 — "T" as Brush Wand The "T" in Tech replaced by a stylized brush-wand bottle; bristles emerge from the cap.
H2 — "i" as Miniature Bottle The "i" in "Nail" replaced by a mini polish bottle; Dusty Rose cap serves as the dot.

Direction I — Small Icon + Full Wordmark Lockup

I1 — Icon + Full Wordmark Compact bottle icon in Mulberry Dusk left of Fraunces Light wordmark; clean horizontal lockup.
I2 — Glass Bottle + Wordmark Semi-transparent glass treatment reveals Dusty Rose liquid inside; Dusty Rose cap detail.

Direction J — Bottle Monogram Container (NTA)

J1 — Outline Bottle + Gold NTA Monogram Mulberry Dusk bottle outline, "NTA" in Champagne Gold Fraunces SemiBold centered inside.
J2 — Filled Bottle + Reversed NTA Monogram Mulberry Dusk filled bottle, Ivory Cream "NTA" reversed out, Dusty Rose cap detail.
