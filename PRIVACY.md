# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-04-17

## Scope

Nail Tech Assistant is a single-user personal application. It has exactly one user: the owner. It is not offered to the public, is not commercially distributed, and does not accept new sign-ups.

## Data accessed via Pinterest

With the user's authorization, the app reads the user's own Pinterest boards and pins for the sole purpose of displaying them inside the app as design inspiration. Pinterest data is not stored beyond what is required to render the selected references within the app. Pinterest data is not shared with third parties, not sold, not used for advertising, not used for analytics, and not used to train any model.

## Image and design data

Reference images (uploaded from the user's device or imported from the user's Pinterest) and AI-generated design outputs are stored in the user's own Supabase project, accessible only by the authenticated user. They are not shared with third parties.

## Third parties

The app calls Pinterest (for the user's own authorized pin data) and Google Generative AI / Gemini (to generate design previews from the user's reference images and prompts). These providers are subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

## Retention

Data is retained indefinitely at the user's discretion. The single user may delete their own records at any time by removing them from the underlying Supabase project.

## Contact

Contact the owner at the email configured as the sole allowlisted address for the app.
